Current approaches try to improve the quality of vaccines or build better roads in rural areas.

Our Solution? Drones.

Our drones fly to the most rural parts of Africa, inaccessible to ground transportation. Vaccines are parachuted down in packages to health workstations. The entire flight is autonomous.
